<objective>
Wire the parsed query AST into the search store, replace substring matching with AST evaluation, and build the bidirectional sync between search bar and sidebar filters.

Purpose: This is the critical integration layer. The search store evolves from a simple string holder to an AST-based query engine. The bidirectional sync hook makes the search bar and sidebar filters two UIs for one truth source (per locked decision). The sync must prevent infinite update loops (per research Pitfall 1).

Output: searchStore with AST, evaluator-based useSearchResults, and loop-safe useQuerySync hook.
</objective>
