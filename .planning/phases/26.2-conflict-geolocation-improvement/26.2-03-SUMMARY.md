## Decisions Made
- CAMEO_TO_FIPS includes both YMN and YEM for Yemen since GDELT uses both actor codes
- ISO_TO_FIPS mapping table bridges lookupCityCoords ISO codes to FIPS geo codes
- Cross-border events validated by place country match, not actor country match (Pitfall 5 from RESEARCH.md)
- Centroid relocation only for ActionGeoType 3/4 (city/landmark), not 1/2 (country/state)
- Confidence threshold conservatively raised to 0.38 (from 0.35) -- avoids over-filtering while tightening
- Removed 180/182/190 from CAMEO_SPECIFICITY since they are hard-excluded before scoring
