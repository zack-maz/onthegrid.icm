## Decisions Made

- **Callback injection over direct import:** processEventGroups and geocodeEnrichedEvents accept optional callbacks rather than importing llmProgress directly. This keeps them pure and testable without module-level mocks, while the events route wires callbacks to the singleton.
- **Module-level singleton over Map:** A single `LLMPipelineProgress` object is simpler than a Map and matches the single-pipeline-per-instance reality. Object.assign provides atomic partial updates.
- **buildSummary derives from live state:** Rather than maintaining a separate summary structure, buildSummary() reads the singleton at completion time. Single source of truth, no drift.
- **Route ordering:** `/llm-status` route registered before the catch-all `/` route on eventsRouter to ensure Express matches it correctly.
