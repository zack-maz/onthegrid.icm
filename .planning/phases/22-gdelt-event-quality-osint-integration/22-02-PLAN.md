    - checkBellingcatCorroboration returns { matched: true, article } when article passes temporal (+-24h) AND geographic (200km) AND keyword (>=2 matches) gates
    - Temporal gate: article publishedAt must be within 24h of event timestamp
    - Geographic gate: article must have lat/lng, distance from event must be <= 200km (haversineKm)
    - Keyword gate: at least 2 words (>=3 chars) from event.data.locationName must appear in article title
    - Articles without lat/lng are rejected at geographic gate (return false)
    - Geographic coordinates for Bellingcat articles are extracted from title by matching against CITY_CENTROIDS names
    - When matched, the boost amount is configurable via getConfig().bellingcatCorroborationBoost (default 0.2)
  </behavior>
  <action>

       - Implement `extractBellingcatGeo(title: string)`: scan title for CITY_CENTROIDS names (case-insensitive), return first match's lat/lng or undefined. This is used by the caller to populate lat/lng on Bellingcat articles before passing them in.
