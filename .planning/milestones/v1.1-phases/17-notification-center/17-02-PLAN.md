    - When isDefaultWindowActive, news clusters are filtered to only those with lastUpdated >= (Date.now() - 24h)
    - When custom range active (isDefaultWindowActive=false), no additional 24h filtering is applied to events or news
    - Flights and ships are NEVER affected by the 24h default window
    - The 24h cutoff recomputes on each render (not cached at a fixed time)
