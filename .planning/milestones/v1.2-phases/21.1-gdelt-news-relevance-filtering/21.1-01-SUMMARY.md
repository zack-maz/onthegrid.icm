## Decisions Made
- **compromise over wink-nlp**: Self-contained (no separate model download), pattern matching syntax enables declarative triple extraction, ~250KB bundle compatible with Vercel serverless
- **3-factor additive scoring model**: Triple completeness (0-0.45) weighted highest for action verb (+0.20), negativity via conflict verb ratio (0-0.35), source reliability tier (0-0.20); factors sum to max 1.0
- **Source reliability 4-tier system**: Major international (1.0), regional quality (0.9-0.95), state-affiliated (0.8), unknown GDELT (0.6 default); lookup by source name first then domain
- **24 conflict verbs for negativity**: Uses compromise verb root normalization (toInfinitive) plus direct token matching for robustness
- **50+ exclusion patterns across 7 categories**: Historical/documentary, entertainment, sports, education, technology, weather/natural added to existing set
