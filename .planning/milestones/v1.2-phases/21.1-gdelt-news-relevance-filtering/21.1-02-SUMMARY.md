## Decisions Made
- **Dual gate design**: Articles must pass BOTH keyword match (>= 1 non-ambiguous term) AND relevance score (>= 0.7). This prevents articles with only geographic/diplomatic terms from passing, while still requiring NLP-level relevance for articles that do have conflict keywords.
- **Exclusion patterns from relevanceScorer**: Rather than duplicating the expanded exclusion patterns in newsFilter, imported EXCLUSION_PATTERNS from relevanceScorer.ts as single source of truth.
- **Backward-compat deprecation**: filterConflictArticles and CONFLICT_KEYWORDS kept as exports but deprecated, delegating to new implementations.
- **Route test fixtures updated**: gdeltArticle2 and rssArticle1 titles updated to include non-ambiguous keywords since the new stricter filter correctly rejects articles with only ambiguous terms.
